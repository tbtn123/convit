Let's gather some resources!

**Mining**: /mine (needs pickaxe, costs 10 energy). Move depths in 5m increments.

**Zones**: Surface→Iron→Gold→Diamond (deeper = better loot).

**Events**: Cave-in (reset), Rich Vein (3x rewards), Gas Pocket (-30 energy), Treasure (free loot).

**Farming**: /farm plant/harvest. Up to 5 farms.

**Crafting**: /craft items, /recipes to view. Create gear from materials.
