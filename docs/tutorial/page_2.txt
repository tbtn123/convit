Let's talk about your core stats!

**Energy**: Your stamina (max 100). Required for work, mining, gambling. Use /health to check it!

**Mood**: Happiness meter (max 100) that affects success rates. High mood = better chances.

**Status Effects**:
- Overworked: Can't work 15min
- Injured: Can't RPG 5min  
- Gambling Addiction: Bigger mood swings
- Motivated/Demoralized: Work bonuses

All effects wear off automatically or can be cured!
