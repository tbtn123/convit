Ready for RPG combat?

**Battles**: /rpg-battle starts adventures with safe zones to prepare.

**Actions**: Attack, Defend (50% damage reduction), Reload ammo, Run (60% escape), Skip peaceful enemies.

**Weapons**: Critical hits, break chance, ammo limits. Fists as backup.

**Effects**: 5min injury cooldown, weapon durability, ammo consumption.

**Rewards**: Materials, weapons, coins from victories.

Safe zones: Use items, change weapons, check loot!
