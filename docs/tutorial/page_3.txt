Let's manage your items and weapons!

**Inventory**: Limited slots - overload gives 25% penalty. Use /inventory to view.

**Weapons**: 
- Fists: Default, no ammo
- Melee: Damage ranges, can break
- Ranged: Need ammo, higher damage

**Ammo**: Limited per magazine, reload in combat.

**Items**: Materials (work/mining), tools (pickaxes), consumables (potions), weapons, seeds.

Equip your best gear before RPG battles!
